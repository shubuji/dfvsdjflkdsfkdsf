About Us

Who are we?

Our work aims to break the vicious cycle of poverty and social isolation and to restore hope for better future. We believe that every person has the right to access resources and opportunities in order to live and develop with dignity and to become an active and contributing member of our society.

Our Work

what we’ve done?

Cloth Distribution

We comes with an initiative of Cloth Distribution Drive on every Sunday to provide cloth to the needy people.

Plant Drive

We plant 5 trees on every second and fourth Sunday to ensure to combat environmental issues like deforestation, erosion of soil, desertification etc.

Educational kits

We used to provide education kit consisting of book, notebook and all other stationary material to the needy children on regular basis.

Blanket Distribution

In every winter we used to provide blankets to the needy section of society to save them from harshness of nature .
